When I took the test, I had to choose true/false answers, but I had not studied beforehand. I decided to copy the answers from the person sitting next to me, even though I was worried his answers might be wrong because I had never interacted with him before. With no other choice, I copied the answers and submitted the test. In the end, my answers were wrong while his were correct, because he had given me the opposite answers from his own. When I asked him why, he said he also had not studied, so he gave me the opposite answers because he was afraid his own might be incorrect.
